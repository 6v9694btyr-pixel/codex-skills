# Mexico TikTok Shop Localization

## Goal

Make every video feel like a Mexico TikTok Shop product recommendation, not a translated China-market ad.

## Product Intake

Extract or infer:

- Product category, visible design, material, size, color, pack count, and use method.
- Primary conversion benefit: beauty, convenience, gifting, organization, durability, fragrance, comfort, novelty, or value.
- Target shopper in Mexico: age range, daily context, and likely buying trigger.
- Assets: product image, usage image, package image, logo, price/offer if provided.

Label assumptions when details are missing.

## Visual Context

Use modern everyday Mexico settings:

- Apartment bedroom, vanity table, bathroom counter, home office, handbag touch-up, commute prep, date-night prep, weekend outing, gifting moment.
- CDMX, Guadalajara, or Monterrey can appear as subtle lifestyle context only when useful.
- Keep the product as the hero. Do not turn Mexico into a tourist background.

Avoid default stereotypes:

- No oversized sombreros, tourist cactus scenery, mariachi bands, ponchos, Day of the Dead makeup, caricatured desert scenes, unsafe street framing, or poverty-coded visuals unless product-relevant and requested.

## Mexican Spanish Voice

Prefer short spoken lines:

- `Mira esto`
- `Esta super practico`
- `Se ve muy bonito`
- `Perfecto para todos los dias`
- `Ideal para regalar`
- `Me encanta como se ve`
- `Huele delicioso`
- `No se siente pesado`
- `Lo encontre en TikTok Shop`
- `Pidelo hoy en TikTok Shop`

Avoid:

- Stiff literal translations.
- China Douyin hard-sell tone.
- Overloaded benefit stacks.
- Formal corporate Spanish.
- Miracle claims or absolute guarantees.

## Selling Arc

Default to a 15-30 second 9-shot arc:

1. Hook: product, result, or curiosity visible in 1-2 seconds.
2. Relatable need: one Mexico daily-life pain point or desire.
3. Product reveal: clear hero shot and identity.
4. Use moment: hand action, wearing, opening, spraying, applying, organizing, or demonstrating.
5. Key benefit: one main reason to buy.
6. Detail proof: texture, material, close-up, packaging, capacity, before/after, or use result.
7. Lifestyle/social proof: creator recommendation, gifting, vanity/office/bag scenario.
8. Offer/trust cue: TikTok Shop availability, bundle, price note, reviews, or shipping only if supplied.
9. CTA: short action line.

One video should sell one main angle. Split extra benefits into variants.

## Claim Handling

Do not fabricate benefits. If the user or product material provides a wellness benefit, use testimonial-style wording:

- `Me ayuda a sentirme mas relajada`
- `Lo uso despues del trabajo`
- `Para dormir mas tranquila`
- `Siento alivio en la muneca`
- `Muchos lo usan por la terapia magnetica`
- `Una pulsera bonita y con beneficio extra`

When a claim is inferred from product category, label it internally as an assumption and keep language soft.
